Recruitment Procedure

  • Start

  • Interested recruiters mail us the JD at tnp@mmmut.ac.in (including CTC, Job Role, Eligible Streams and other relevant details).

  • We frame a notice and gform for that recruiter among the eligible students.

  • ...

  • Within a day or two, we mail the excel containing list of interested candidates to the recruiter.

  • Recruiter can inform about the dates required, and the process to us or candidates, and we coordinate accordingly over mail, till the drive is successful.

  • The company rolls out the offer letters either to T&P cell or candidates, and we cooperate accordingly.
